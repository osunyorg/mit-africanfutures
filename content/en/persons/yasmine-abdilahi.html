Yasmine Abdillahi is Executive Director in Global Cybersecurity, leading Security Risk & Compliance across Comcast and Sky. She's a recognized industry voice at CISO forums including SINET and Gartner Evanta, and a featured BrightTalk speaker on Cyber GRC. Yasmine also serves as a Nonresident Fellow at the Atlantic Council's Africa Center, where she leads work on Cyber and Digital Resilience.
