The African Futures Lab raises public awareness of global racial injustices and empowers civil society actors, as well as public and private entities, to demand justice and achieve reform.

Our Thematic Priorities
At African Futures Lab, racial justice is intersectional.We focus on the structural harms that disproportionately affect African and Afro-descendant communities—especially Black women and climate-vulnerable populations. Explore our two flagship programs:

Gender Justice

Our challenge: Colonial legacies still drive social, political and economic inequalities between Europe & Africa, yet international development actors ignore these systemic racial injustices.
 We refuse to accept the status quo. African Futures Lab will: - Expose hidden racial power plays. - Amplify communities most affected. - Demand bold policy reforms.
