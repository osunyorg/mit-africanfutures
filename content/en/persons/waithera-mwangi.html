Waithera Mwangi is a finance and operations professional with over eight years experience supporting mission-driven organisations across Africa, Europe and North America.

Her work focuses on building strong financial, operational and governance systems that enable organisations to deliver impact sustainably. Her experience spans grant management, financial planning, compliance, and organizational development across multi-country teams, strengthening accountability. Waithera has worked with a wide range of nonprofit and social impact organizations, supporting them to move from reactive operations to more structured systems that support long-term growth.

Outside of work, Waithera enjoys watching clouds, observing people, coaching, and engaging with philosophical reflections on life & meaning.
