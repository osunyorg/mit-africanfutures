Anna is a senior fundraising leader with extensive experience in global partnerships, community engagement, and Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI). Currently, she serves as the Associate Director of Corporate Partnerships Development at HEC Paris, whereby she utilizes her expertise to foster collaboration and deepen engagements in the institution's strategic mission.

Previously at INSEAD, Anna played an instrumental role in the success of the €311 million Capital Campaign, in doing so strengthening alumni engagement worldwide. She led donor strategies, global gift portfolios, and alumni initiatives that significantly expanded the school's philanthropic reach. In addition to her professional achievements, Anna is a committed mentor, guiding women entrepreneurs and emerging professionals to reach their goals. Her consulting background further enhances her ability to lead high-impact projects, navigate cross-cultural contexts, and drive organisational change.

Anna is convinced of the transformative power of education and lifelong learning to advance both social and business change. Her leadership and expertise bring invaluable strength to Afalab as we continue to champion racial justice, reparatory justice, and structural reform across Africa and Europe.
