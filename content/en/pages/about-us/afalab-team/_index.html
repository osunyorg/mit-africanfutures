African Futures Lab is led by a transcontinental team of researchers, advocates, and strategists united by a shared commitment to dismantling global racial injustice. From Brussels to Nairobi, from legal scholars to campaigners, our staff and board members bring deep expertise, lived experience, and unwavering conviction to the fight for structural reform and reparative justice.Together, we work across disciplines and borders to produce knowledge, influence policy, and empower communities.
