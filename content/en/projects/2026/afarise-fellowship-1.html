In 2025,  we launched the AfaRise programme, recognizing the importance of working alongside amplifiers and mission-driven individuals. The inaugural cohort brought together fellows whose work confronts racial injustice as a structural and global reality shaped by colonialism, extractivism, and contemporary systems of exclusion.
