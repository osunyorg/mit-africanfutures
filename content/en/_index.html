Save the date!
African Symposium on Climate Reparations The African Climate Reparations Collective is pleased to announce the African Symposium on Climate Reparations: Accountability, Equity, and Repair for People and Planet, taking place on 30 September – 1 October 2026 in Mombasa, Kenya. At a time when climate impacts are intensifying across Africa and among Afro-descendant communities worldwide, the demand for climate reparations is gaining unprecedented political, legal, and moral relevance. This symposium will bring together leaders, practitioners, and advocates working at the intersection of climate justice, reparations, biodiversity, human rights, and community-led solutions to collectively shape a shared vision for climate reparations.  We will share more details soon.
