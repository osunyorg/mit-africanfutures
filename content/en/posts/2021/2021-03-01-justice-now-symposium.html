Organized in March 2021,  the Justice Now? symposium  gathered speakers in areas such as transitional justice, racial justice, museum, legal, colonial and Decolonial studies discussed experiences and shared strategies in the development of anti-racist and decolonial movements. The symposium was convened by the  Anthropology department at MIT  in collaboration with international human rights NGO  Avocats Sans Frontières .
