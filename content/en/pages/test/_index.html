“Once upon a time — In just 50 years, we will have dismantled the global system of racial domination. What was once a banal, unquestioned status quo — where the lives of African and Afro-descendant people were devalued and oppression normalized — will be nothing more than a distant memory. Instead, our dignity and rights will not only be protected but celebrated. We will be honored for the sacrifices we made to bring humanity to this pivotal moment, and we will receive the compassionate political and social support we deserve, around the world.”
This future may sound utopian today, but every action—big or small—and the unwavering solidarity of our community make it possible.

Our Partners

What is a MobLab?

Where knowledge becomes action. MobLabs are collaborative spaces where activists, researchers, and institutions come together to design bold strategies for racial justice.They turn shared knowledge into collective action-through campaigns, reforms, and long-term alliances.

Gender justice

Centering Black women in the fight for justice. AfaLab exposes and resists gendered colonial violence through research, advocacy, and action.

Climate justice

Exposing colonial roots of the climate crisis. AfaLab fights for climate reparations led by African voices, grounded in justice and accountability.

AFaLab Campaigns

From Voices to Action. AFaLab campaigns turn collective expertise into action. Built from our Mobilization Labs, they unite diverse voices to challenge racial injustice and push for structural reform across Africa and Europe.
